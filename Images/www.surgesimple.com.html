Charge Providers

Charge Providers

SurgeSimple can help you manage the use of your Electric Vehicle (EV) charging station network. We understand that often when companies or governmental organizations purchase charging stations for their drivers, they become concerned about managing the chargers and handling driver issues. Dedicating staff to deal with EV driver problems can be complicated and expensive. SurgeSimple provides a simple way to streamline charging station usage by optimizing the user experience while lessening your responsibility.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Helping the world embrace the electric vehicle revolution.

How much does SurgeSimple cost?

The SurgeSimple app is totally free for users in Apple iTunes and Google Play Store. Since we offer customized options and try to keep our pricing as competitive as possible, we ask that businesses contact our sales team for pricing and further information.

Who is this service for?

SurgeSimple is for businesses that are interested in providing charging solutions for their electric vehicle drivers. Examples include Corporate Offices, Universities, Apartment Complexes, etc.

Why should businesses get SurgeSimple?

With SurgeSimple, businesses are able to manage the use of their charging stations without dedicating additional staff. With green solutions like SurgeSimple, they are also able to advance their sustainability goals and improve their corporate image.

Why should Electric Vehicle drivers use SurgeSimple?

There’s nothing worse than getting to your destination with very little juice left and realizing that all the charging stations are already taken. You have no idea how long those cars will be there and no way of knowing when they will leave. Now, SurgeSimple provides easy scheduling and allows open communication amongst EV drivers.

How can I request SurgeSimple be added to a charging station near me?

Its Simple! We love driver suggestions and feedback. Just email us at support@surgesimple.com and tell us where we’re needed. We’ll take it from there!
